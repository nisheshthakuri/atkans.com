Top Wonder of Nepal- Ghandruk

Situated in Gandaki Pradesh and a part to Lumbini Province, Nawalparasi was one of the 75 districts of Nepal before being divided. A land of 6 Lakhs population holds the fragrance of the bygone era which is famous for its religious significance such as: Triveni and Sashwatdham.Though rural outskirts of Nawalparasi might not have the most tantalizing architecture, equipment or techniques, they do have a very strong cultural practice stemming from generation after generation, because they know how to create, preserve and continue following the rules of nature and association which is on a very close knit.Â 

No complication, no hustle bustle. Life in Nawalparasi continues and ends every day in a rhythmic manner, which is definitely an inspiration in itself. The astonishing villages of Nawalparsi plunges into utter tranquility and amid in the mesmerizing verdant exquisiteness of nature experiencing the rigors of Sugarcane & paddy cultivation. No matter how small, fragile or rustic their home is, the people there are always welcoming & making us drool for their exotic delicacies. Warmth like this is definitely a nudity of soul which cannot be experienced, owned or shared through monetary exchange. And although we can find differentiability in the culture, their hospitality touched our heart and their kindness touched our soul for it was so difficult to bid adieu.

Thus, for each one of us, how a Nepali Village speaks to us will be a completely different and personal experience. But what matters, more than anything, is that all of it will surely touch your heart and leave an indelible mark.
