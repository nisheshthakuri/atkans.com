Top Wonder of Nepal- Ghandruk

Surrounded by a series of soaring, craggy hills and a set on the idyllic lake Phewa, Pokhara is a wonderful mix of ornate temples, stately hotels, narrow whitewashed streets and picturesque lakeside full of delightful cafes. A small quaint town frequented by locals and tourists to enjoy serenity, solace and peace. The bewildering beauty of imposing Himalayas and intriguing Phewa Lake is what one can experience and be in awe.  

Hills and hearts start with the same letter and perhaps that is why hills are so close to everyone’s heart. The view from the top, the lush city, the ambrosial lakes is a blessing in disguise for this city is filled with everything magical around. Pokhara has each and every ingredient which can relish its visitors. Stupas, museums, temples, hills, forests, statutes, beautiful hotels, malls, lakes and what not. This city serves abundance of wildest adventure and a perfect place for someone who has zeal for thrills. Beyond the tangible beauty of the town is a lively art scene, pristine lakes, stupas, colorful lakeside bazaar. The charming beauty of paradisiacal Pokhara is something that not just takes your breath away but refreshes and rejuvenates your mind, body and senses.  

The breathtaking sunset views, lakes and water bodies, entrancing greenery, adorable temples and exquisite mountain views are something which will make for long lasting memory. This city has the power to make us ease all of our worries and feel like this is the only place that we truly belong to.
