About Us

Great Ventures start from small beginnings.

We will let the idea behind the company describe what it is really about!

Let me introduce you to a peculiar group of storytellers and thinkers. This group doesn’t want to be considered the next big thing in the global market. Rather it wants to be the first storytellers of the blogging platform. I am “this” group. I am the idea of the innovators, thinkers, and storytellers. I am created to connect every single individual of this country. And, I am not going to do it by hook or crook but through stories.

Let me walk you back to when I was created. It was a story that birthed me. A story told by a two friends who dreamt to connect Nepal to the global platform. This story soon became an idea and subsequently, I was recognized. I got the name “ATKANS”.

I was designed to tell the story of Nepal. I am programmed differently -- I’ve been told by the thinkers that it’s not the product or brand name that connects people but stories narrated from the sweaty palms and cheerful eyes. I am programmed to connect societies of this beautiful country. The stories that run between my code have given me the perfect image of how this country is divided in terms of development.

We work together to design, create, and produce work that we are proud of for what we believe in.
